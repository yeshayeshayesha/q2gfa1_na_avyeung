UNITED ARAB EMIRATES: DUBAI

Dubai is the largest city in the United Arab Emirates and a major global hub for tourism, commerce, and finance. Known for its modern architecture, luxurious lifestyle, and dynamic economy, Dubai is a vibrant cosmopolitan city that seamlessly blends traditional Arabic culture with a contemporary flair. The city is famous for its iconic landmarks such as the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, Palm Jumeirah, an artificial island archipelago, and the Burj Al Arab, a luxury hotel shaped like a sail. Dubai offers a plethora of attractions, including extravagant shopping malls, stunning beaches, world-class restaurants, and thrilling recreational activities like desert safaris and indoor skiing. With its diverse population, Dubai is a melting pot of different cultures and traditions, providing a unique and multicultural experience for visitors.
